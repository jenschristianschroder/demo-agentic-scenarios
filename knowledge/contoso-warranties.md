# Contoso Electronics — Warranty & Support Programs 2025

## Overview

Contoso Electronics offers tiered warranty and support programs designed for consumers, businesses, and education institutions. All warranties are backed by Contoso's European service network with certified repair centers in 14 countries.

## Warranty Tiers

### Standard Carry-In Warranty

- **Duration:** 1–2 years depending on product
- **Coverage:** Manufacturing defects, hardware failures, battery degradation below 80% capacity
- **Service model:** Customer ships device to nearest Contoso-certified repair center
- **Turnaround:** 5 business days average from receipt to return
- **Parts:** Genuine Contoso replacement parts included at no cost
- **Exclusions:** Accidental damage, liquid damage, unauthorized modifications, cosmetic wear
- **Products covered:** Contoso AirBook S5 (2 years), Contoso Tab Pro 12 (2 years), Contoso Tab Go 8 (1 year)

### Business On-Site Warranty

- **Duration:** 3 years
- **Coverage:** Manufacturing defects, hardware failures, battery degradation below 80% capacity
- **Service model:** Contoso-certified technician dispatched to customer's business location
- **Response time:** Next business day for metropolitan areas; within 2 business days for other locations
- **Hours of support:** Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00 local time
- **Parts:** Genuine Contoso replacement parts included at no cost
- **Loaner program:** Temporary replacement device available within 24 hours if repair exceeds 4 hours on-site
- **Products covered:** Contoso ProBook X1

### Business On-Site with Accidental Damage Protection

- **Duration:** 3 years
- **Coverage:** Everything in Business On-Site, plus accidental damage from drops, spills, electrical surges, and cracked screens
- **Claims limit:** Up to 2 accidental damage claims per year
- **Deductible:** DKK 500 (€67) per accidental damage claim
- **Service model:** Same as Business On-Site — next-business-day technician dispatch
- **Products covered:** Contoso ProBook X1 Max, Contoso StudioBook 17

### Education Replacement Warranty

- **Duration:** 3 years
- **Coverage:** Manufacturing defects, hardware failures, battery degradation below 80% capacity
- **Service model:** Next-business-day device replacement — Contoso ships a replacement unit and customer returns the defective device
- **Advance replacement:** Pre-configured replacement device shipped overnight
- **Bulk support:** Dedicated account manager for orders of 50+ units
- **Products covered:** Contoso EduBook E3

## Warranty Extension Programs

### Contoso Care+ (Consumer)

- **Eligibility:** Any Contoso product with standard carry-in warranty
- **Extension:** Adds 1 or 2 additional years of coverage
- **Pricing:** 10% of product retail price per additional year
- **Purchase window:** Within 90 days of original product purchase
- **Additional benefit:** Adds accidental damage protection (1 claim per year, DKK 300 deductible)

### Contoso Business Pro Care

- **Eligibility:** Contoso ProBook X1, ProBook X1 Max, StudioBook 17
- **Extension:** Extends on-site warranty to 4 or 5 years
- **Pricing:** DKK 1,200 per additional year (ProBook X1), DKK 1,800 per additional year (ProBook X1 Max, StudioBook 17)
- **Purchase window:** Within 30 days of original product purchase or at time of order
- **Additional benefits:**
  - Priority phone support with 30-minute callback guarantee
  - Annual device health check by certified technician
  - Advance parts stocking at customer location for fleets of 100+ units

### Contoso Education Fleet Care

- **Eligibility:** Contoso EduBook E3 orders of 25+ units
- **Extension:** Extends replacement warranty to 4 years
- **Pricing:** DKK 350 per unit per additional year
- **Additional benefits:**
  - Dedicated education support hotline (Monday–Friday, 07:00–20:00)
  - Pre-imaged replacement devices matching school's software configuration
  - End-of-life recycling program at no additional cost

## Business Suitability Guidance

### Recommended for Business Deployment

Products with on-site warranty coverage and Windows 11 Pro are recommended for enterprise and business deployments:

- **Contoso ProBook X1** — 3-year on-site warranty, Windows 11 Pro, next-business-day response. Ideal for general business use, field sales, and corporate fleets.
- **Contoso ProBook X1 Max** — 3-year on-site with accidental damage protection, Windows 11 Pro, dedicated GPU. Recommended for power users, engineers, and executives.
- **Contoso StudioBook 17** — 3-year on-site with accidental damage protection, Windows 11 Pro. Best for creative professionals and engineering workstations.

### Acceptable for Business with Considerations

- **Contoso EduBook E3** — 3-year replacement warranty with next-business-day replacement is fast, but the device runs Windows 11 SE which limits application compatibility. Best for kiosk, frontline, or single-purpose deployments.

### Not Recommended for Business Deployment

- **Contoso AirBook S5** — Only 2-year carry-in warranty, Windows 11 Home (not Pro), no on-site service. Consumer-grade support is insufficient for business SLAs.
- **Contoso Tab Pro 12** — 2-year carry-in warranty, Android OS, no enterprise management tools. Not suitable for Windows-based business environments.
- **Contoso Tab Go 8** — 1-year carry-in warranty, Android OS, consumer-only support. Not recommended for any business use.

## Warranty Claim Process

1. **Register warranty** at support.contoso-electronics.com within 30 days of purchase
2. **Contact support** via phone or online portal to log a warranty claim
3. **Provide proof of purchase** — invoice or order confirmation
4. **Diagnostic assessment** — remote or on-site depending on warranty tier
5. **Service execution** — repair, replacement, or parts dispatch within SLA timeframes
6. **Confirmation** — customer receives service report and updated warranty status
