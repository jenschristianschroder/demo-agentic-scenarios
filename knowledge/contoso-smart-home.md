## Setup & Installation

### Quick Start (Small Apartment)

1. Plug in the Home Hub and connect to Wi-Fi
2. Open the Contoso Home app and create an account
3. Pair the Home Hub by scanning the QR code on the bottom
4. Add sensors: press the pairing button on each sensor and the hub auto-discovers them
5. Add smart bulbs: screw in, power on, and the hub discovers them automatically via Zigbee
6. Configure automations in the app (e.g., "turn on lights when motion detected")
7. Optional: enable local processing mode for maximum privacy

### Estimated Setup Time

- Hub setup: 5 minutes
- Sensor kit pairing: 10 minutes
- Smart bulbs (4-pack): 5 minutes
- Basic automations: 10 minutes
- **Total: approximately 30 minutes**
