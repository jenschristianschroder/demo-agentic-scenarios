## Volume Discount Tiers

Contoso offers tiered volume discounts based on the total number of units in a single order. Discounts apply to all products in the order.

| Quantity | Discount |
|----------|----------|
| 1–9 units | 0% (MSRP) |
| 10–24 units | 3% off MSRP |
| 25–49 units | 5% off MSRP |
| 50–99 units | 8% off MSRP |
| 100–249 units | 12% off MSRP |
| 250–499 units | 15% off MSRP |
| 500+ units | 18% off MSRP |

**Note:** Volume discounts are calculated on the per-unit MSRP price and applied to each unit in the order. Mixed-product orders combine all units to determine the discount tier.

## Special Pricing Programs

### Education Pricing

- **Eligibility:** Accredited K-12 schools, universities, and educational institutions
- **Discount:** Additional 10% off volume-discounted price on EduBook E3
- **Minimum order:** 25 units
- **Proof required:** Valid institutional purchase order or education verification

### Government & Public Sector Pricing

- **Eligibility:** Government agencies, municipalities, and public sector organizations
- **Discount:** Additional 8% off volume-discounted price on ProBook X1 and ProBook X1 Max
- **Framework agreements:** Available for contracts exceeding DKK 500,000 annually
- **Procurement:** Compatible with EU public procurement frameworks

### Partner & Reseller Pricing

- **Eligibility:** Authorized Contoso resellers and IT partners
- **Margin:** 20–25% below MSRP depending on partner tier
- **Deal registration:** Additional 3% discount for registered opportunities

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms

- **Payment method:** Invoice, credit card, or purchase order
- **Net terms:** Net 30 days for business accounts with approved credit
- **Currency:** DKK (Danish Krone) or EUR (Euro) — pricing locked at order date

### Volume Order Terms

- **Orders over DKK 100,000:** Net 45 days available
- **Orders over DKK 500,000:** Net 60 days with approved credit and signed framework agreement
- **Leasing:** Available through Contoso Financial Services — 24, 36, or 48-month terms

## Pricing Calculation Rules

1. **Start with MSRP** per unit for each product
2. **Apply volume discount** based on total units in order (all products combined)
3. **Apply special program discount** (education, government) if eligible — calculated on the already volume-discounted price
4. **Add accessories** at listed prices (accessories do not count toward volume discount tier but do receive volume discount if applicable)
5. **Apply bundle savings** if a qualifying bundle is selected
6. **Calculate total** = (discounted unit price × quantity) + accessories − bundle savings
7. **Compare to budget** to determine budget fit

## Currency Conversion

- All prices are listed in DKK (Danish Krone) as the primary currency
- EUR prices are provided as reference at an approximate rate of DKK 7.46 per EUR
- Final invoicing in EUR uses the ECB reference rate on the date of order confirmation
