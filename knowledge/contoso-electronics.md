# Contoso Electronics — Product Catalog 2025

## Company Overview

Contoso Electronics is a consumer electronics company founded in 2003 and headquartered in Copenhagen, Denmark. The company specializes in laptops, tablets, and smart home devices. Contoso operates in 14 countries across Europe and North America with approximately 2,800 employees. Annual revenue for fiscal year 2024 was DKK 4.2 billion (approximately €563 million).
